About Sulaymaniyah

Sulaymaniyah is a vibrant city located in the Kurdistan Region of Iraq. Known for its rich culture, history, and stunning landscapes, it is a must-visit destination for travelers.

Top Attractions

Goizha Mountain

Enjoy breathtaking views and hiking trails at Goizha Mountain, a popular spot for nature enthusiasts.

Sulaymaniyah Museum

Explore the history and culture of the region at the Sulaymaniyah Museum, home to fascinating artifacts.

Azadi Park

Relax and unwind at Azadi Park, a beautiful green space in the heart of the city.
