About Duhok

Duhok is a beautiful city located in the Kurdistan Region of Iraq. It is known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality.

Explore the city's historical sites, enjoy its vibrant markets, and experience the unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Things to Do

Visit the Duhok Dam, enjoy the breathtaking views of the mountains, and immerse yourself in the local culture by trying traditional Kurdish cuisine.
